# Product

## Positioning

Household Intelligence is a decision engine for busy households.

Not a dashboard.
Not a generic AI assistant.
Not another to-do app.

It converts household signals into clear, ranked actions.

## Core promise

In under 10 seconds, a household should know:

- what matters right now
- who owns it
- why now
- what can safely wait

## Initial users

- Home Assistant households
- families with logistics overload
- energy-aware homes
- highly technical early adopters
- ADHD / high-chaos households

## Initial outputs

- Top 3 Things Today
- daily brief
- Nikolaj tasks / person-specific tasks
- one key risk
- one opportunity window
- one can-wait callout

## Why this wins

Most competitors stop at visibility.
This product should push into decision quality.
